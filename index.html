My Journey to Become a Data Scientist

Hi! Welcome to my webpage. I am Fahmina, in my mid 30s, a mom, an accounts officer by profession and I am in the path of changing my accounting career to be a Data Scientist.

Coming from a non-technical background, I initially struggled to find out what I have to do to become a data scientist. I watched many youtube videos, read several articles, but, I was still not sure where to start! I bumped into several Data Science bootcamps and university degrees, but, I did not have the means to bear the expenses.

I started writing here, to inspire people like me who is not sure where to and how to start their data science journey.

After several months of researching, I believe I am in a position, to share my insight and journey with you, in order to motivate you; to guide you.

I will post weekly journals to update you with my progress and how far I have come since I started my journey; also, what is my next step towards achieving my goal.


We always here the success stories, but we do not see the process or struggle that one has to go through to achieve the success.

In these blogs we will embark on our journey together towards our similar career goal of becoming a Data Scientist / Data Analyst.

So, if you are like me, trying to become a data scientist but not getting a sense of direction, then

welcome aboard.
